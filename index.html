Space Mission · 天宫二号

Artwork Launched into Orbit

Marching to Victory — Tiangong-2

The joint calligraphy-and-painting work Marching to Victory, co-created with Mao Xueqin, was launched aboard Tiangong-2 in September 2016. Retrieved two months later by Shenzhou-11, it was formally acquired by a PLA institution. Zhang and his wife attended the launch at Beijing Flight Control Centre.

Great Hall of the People · 人民大会堂

First Mao-Style Work Ever Collected

Bonds of the People & the Army — D2273

On 30 May 2024, the calligraphy Bonds of the People and the Army was formally acquired by the Administration Bureau of the Great Hall of the People, assigned accession number D2273. It is the first Mao-style calligraphy work to enter the institution's permanent collection.

The Calligrapher · 书法家

张满堂
Zhang Mantang

"In every stroke, decades of devotion. In every composition, the spirit of an era."

Born in 1963 in Binzhou, Shandong, Zhang Mantang encountered Mao Zedong's calligraphy in the journals Qiushi and Hongqi in the early 1990s and has devoted himself to the art ever since. Under masters Duan Chenggui and Xie Tiancheng, he developed a style both faithful to the source and deeply personal.

He serves as Vice Chairman of the China Mao Zedong Calligraphy Research Association and guest professor at Tsinghua University, Jinan University, and Binzhou University. Over the years he has funded more than 200 students from impoverished families through the proceeds of his calligraphy.

Career Milestones · 成就历程

Four Decades of Recognition

1990s
Encountered Mao Zedong's calligraphy in Qiushi and Hongqi; began dedicated study under masters Duan Chenggui and Xie Tiancheng.
2007
Joint exhibition of 114 works at the Military Museum of the Chinese People's Revolution with Mao Xinyu; all auction proceeds donated to students in need.
2009
Works exhibited in Tokyo's China-Japan Friendship Cultural Exchange; awarded Cultural Ambassador title for Japan-China Exchange.
2010
Co-exhibited 97 works with wife Mao Xueqin at Shanghai Library; Gold Award and China-Singapore Cultural Ambassador title.
2013
120-work joint exhibition at Beijing Military Museum for the 120th anniversary of Mao Zedong's birth.
2016 · Sept
Two calligraphy works launched aboard Tiangong-2; retrieved by Shenzhou-11 and formally acquired by PLA institution. Artist and wife attended the launch ceremony at Beijing Flight Control Centre.
